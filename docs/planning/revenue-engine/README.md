# Revenue Engine Planning Documents

These are business planning documents describing the commercial direction for
PlatformKit.

The controlling positioning is:

1. PlatformKit is the flagship commercial product.
2. Septagon is the parent company that develops and supports PlatformKit.
3. COMUM, Incomum, Apex, and Velora are product lines or customer products
   built on PlatformKit, not disposable demos.

- [`product-portfolio-readiness.md`](./product-portfolio-readiness.md) - what
  each product must prove before it is presented as end-to-end ready.
- [`product-portfolio-gap-analysis.md`](./product-portfolio-gap-analysis.md) -
  client-by-client requirements and gaps for COMUM, Incomum, Apex, and Velora.
- [`revenue-engine-program.md`](./revenue-engine-program.md) - commercial
  milestones and workstreams.
- [`revenue-engine-backlog.md`](./revenue-engine-backlog.md) - M0/M1 execution
  backlog.
