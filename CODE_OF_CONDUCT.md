# Code of Conduct

We expect all participation in the platformkit project to be respectful,
constructive, and professional.

## Our standards

- Be clear, factual, and respectful.
- Assume good intent, but challenge ideas rigorously.
- Focus on actionable feedback and reproducible problems.
- Keep discussions relevant to the repository and the users it serves.
- Do not harass, intimidate, discriminate, or publish private information.

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, remove, or lock content that violates this code of
conduct. They may also restrict participation when repeated or severe violations
make the community unsafe or unproductive.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers
through the support or security paths documented in this repository. For
sensitive reports, prefer the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
